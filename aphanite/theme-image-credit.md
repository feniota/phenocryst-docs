# Credits of Mods Used in Aphanite Theme Image

![Aphanite Theme Image](/assets/theme.avif)

The Aphanite Theme Image (as seen on the login page) is an in-game screenshot taken in Minecraft 1.21.1, with various mods added. They are:

## Appearance

- [Photon Shaders](https://modrinth.com/shader/photon-shader) for the excellent lighting and the general atmosphere.
  - [Sodium](https://modrinth.com/mod/sodium) and [Iris Shaders](https://modrinth.com/mod/iris) made custom shaders possible.
- [Ashen 16x](https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/ashen) for the fantastic medieval Minecraft look.
- [Voxy](https://modrinth.com/mod/voxy) (and [m3t4f1v3's NeoForge fork](https://github.com/m3t4f1v3/voxy)) LoD for the incredibly far view distance.
- [Hyper Realistic Sky](https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/hyper-realistic-sky) for the hyper realistic sky.

## Structures

Some structures shown in this screenshot are added by the following mods:

- [TongDa Railway](https://github.com/Hello-LuckyHuang/TongDaRailway) for the bridge and the railway station.
  - [Create](https://modrinth.com/mod/create) and [Create: Steams 'n' Rails](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-steam-n-rails) for the rails on it.
- [Create: Structures Arise](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-structures-arise) for the copper statue at the foot of the mountain and the flying ship at the right edge of the image.
- [Create: Sky Village](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-sky-village) for the floating island at the right edge.
- [Integrated Dungeons and Structures](https://modrinth.com/mod/idas) for the tower.
- [Supplementaries](https://modrinth.com/mod/supplementaries) for the Galleon ship at the right edge of the image.

## Biomes and Blocks

It's difficult to tell the specific mod by which a part is added, so here simply lists them.

- [Regions Unexplored](https://modrinth.com/mod/regions-unexplored).
- [Nature's Spirit](https://modrinth.com/mod/natures-spirit). Most of flowers in the image come from this.
- [Quark](https://modrinth.com/mod/quark).

Also, thank [Tectonic](https://modrinth.com/datapack/tectonic) for the fantastic terrain generation, creating the epic and realistic feeling.

## Miscellaneous

- [NeoForge](https://neoforged.net/) modloader.
- [Sinytra Connector](https://modrinth.com/mod/connector) to load Fabric mods (especially Voxy) on NeoForge.
